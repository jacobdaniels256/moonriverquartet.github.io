STRINGS FOR YOUR FAVORITE MEMORIES

We're the Moon River Quartet, a group of student musicians at UVA. We specialize in bringing a personal touch to your wedding or formal event, working closely with you to curate the perfect playlist.

Members: Alexander Bills (Violin), Caleb Wang (Violin), Luke Benfer (Viola), Jacob Daniels (Cello)
